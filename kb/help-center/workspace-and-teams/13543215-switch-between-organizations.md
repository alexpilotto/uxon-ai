## Article
You can switch between organizations from your profile menu.

### How to switch organizations

1. Go to the top right of the app where you see your profile and email address.
2. Click the profile dropdown menu.
3. Under **Organizations**, select the organization you want to switch to.

The organization you are currently using is marked with a **Current** tag.

#### Notes

- If you have been invited to another organization but have not accepted yet, it will appear with a **Pending invite** tag.
