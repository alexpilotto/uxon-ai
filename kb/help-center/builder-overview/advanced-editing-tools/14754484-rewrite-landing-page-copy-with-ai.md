## Article
Use AI copy tools in Builder when you need faster copy iteration without leaving your landing page workflow.

## Generate AI suggestions for a specific element

1. Select a text element in the canvas (for example headline, paragraph, or rich text).
2. Click the **Sparkles** icon (**Open AI copy suggestions**).
3. Optionally set language, model, and extra context.
4. Review suggestions and apply the best option.

## Rewrite copy for a full section

1. Select the section.
2. Click the section **Sparkles** icon (**Rewrite section copy with AI**).
3. Choose whether to include button copy.
4. Generate and review output, then keep what works.

## Tips for better AI output

- Give clear context: audience, offer, and angle.
- Use one message goal per section.
- Compare options in preview before finalizing.

> AI drafts are starting points. Keep your final copy aligned with your brand voice and campaign objective.

## Troubleshooting

**The AI icon is not available on this field.**

- AI suggestions are available on supported text fields only.

**The generated copy feels generic.**

- Add more context in the prompt and regenerate.
