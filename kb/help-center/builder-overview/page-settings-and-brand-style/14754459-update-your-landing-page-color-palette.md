## Article
Use global colour settings to keep your landing page design aligned with your brand and campaign message.

## Open colour settings

1. Open your landing page in Builder.
2. Select **Settings** -> **Colours**.

## Update your core colours

- **Page background**: main page canvas colour.
- **Light background** and **Dark background**: supporting surfaces across sections.
- **Headlines** and **Body text**: core text readability colours.
- **Star rating**: highlight colour for review/social-proof elements.

## Palette tips for marketers

- Keep strong contrast between text and backgrounds for readability.
- Use one primary accent colour to guide CTA attention.
- Match landing page colours with ad creative for a smoother post-click experience.

> Small colour changes can strongly affect conversion. Test one direction at a time so results are easier to interpret.

## Troubleshooting

**Text is hard to read after colour updates.**

- Increase contrast between text and background colours.

**Some elements did not update.**

- Check whether that section or element has its own custom colour override.
