## Article
Tab attention helps you recover distracted visitors by rotating short messages in the browser tab when they leave your page.

## Open Tab attention settings

1. Open your landing page in Builder.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Open **Tab attention**.

## Configure your tab messages

1. Enable **Enable tab attention**.
2. Set **First message** and **Second message**.
3. Set **Message delay** in seconds.
4. Save and test by switching to another tab.

## Message best practices

- Keep each message short and clear.
- Focus on value, not pressure.
- Match your campaign offer language.

> This feature is most effective when your offer is strong and your CTA is clear. Tab messages should support the page, not replace it.

## Troubleshooting

**I do not see message changes.**

- Confirm tab attention is enabled and both messages are filled in.
- Switch to another tab to trigger the behavior.

**The message changes too fast or too slow.**

- Adjust **Message delay** and retest.
