## Article
Use global logo settings to keep your brand identity consistent across landing page sections that pull from shared brand assets.

## Where logo settings are in Builder

1. Open your landing page in Builder.
2. Select **Settings** in the left sidebar.
3. Open **Brand assets**.

## Upload light and dark logo versions

1. In **Logo (light mode)**, upload the version designed for light backgrounds.
2. In **Logo (dark mode)**, upload the version designed for dark backgrounds.
3. Save your changes.

SVG is recommended for sharper results across screen sizes.

## When to use each logo version

- **Light mode logo**: best for white or light section backgrounds.
- **Dark mode logo**: best for dark sections where a light logo improves contrast.

> Best practice: upload both versions before building so templates can stay visually consistent while you iterate.

## Troubleshooting

**My logo does not change in a section.**

- Some sections may use a custom image instead of global logo settings. Open that section's settings and check the logo/image field.

**My logo looks blurry.**

- Use an SVG or a higher-resolution source image for better clarity.
