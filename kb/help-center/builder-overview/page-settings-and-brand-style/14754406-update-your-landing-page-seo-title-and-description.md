## Article
Use this guide to update SEO basics for a landing page variant directly in Builder.

## Where SEO settings are in Builder

1. Open your landing page variant in Builder.
2. Select **Settings** in the left toolbar.
3. In **Page Settings**, open the **SEO** section.

## Update your SEO fields

- **Allow indexing**: turn this on when you want search engines to index the page.
- **Page title**: write a clear, benefit-led headline that matches user intent.
- **Meta description**: summarize the offer and include a strong reason to click.

## When to enable or disable indexing

- **Enable indexing** for evergreen pages you want discoverable in search.
- **Disable indexing** for short-term campaign pages or private testing variants.

> Best practice: keep SEO copy aligned with your ad message and hero section so visitors see a consistent promise from click to page.

## Troubleshooting

**I updated SEO fields but search results still show old text.**

- Search engines can take time to refresh snippets after updates.

**My page should not appear in search.**

- Make sure **Allow indexing** is turned off for that variant.
