## Article
Typography settings in Builder let you create a consistent brand look while keeping your page easy to read on desktop and mobile.

## Open typography settings

1. Open your landing page in Builder.
2. Go to **Settings** -> **Typography**.

## Update headings and body text

- **Headings**: choose font family, weight, line height, text transform, and letter spacing.
- **Body text**: choose font family, weight, desktop/mobile size, line height, and letter spacing.

## Set navigation font style

- Choose whether navigation uses **Body** or **Heading** font source.
- Set navigation weight and optional uppercase style.

## Readability tips for better conversion

- Keep body copy easy to scan, especially on mobile.
- Use heading weight and spacing to create clear hierarchy.
- Avoid mixing too many type styles on one page.

> After changing typography, always check **Mobile preview** before saving.

## Troubleshooting

**Text feels cramped on mobile.**

- Increase **Body text font size (mobile)** and review line height.

**Typography looks inconsistent across sections.**

- Confirm section-level custom styles are not overriding global typography settings.
