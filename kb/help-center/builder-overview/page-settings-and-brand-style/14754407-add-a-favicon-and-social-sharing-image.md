## Article
Brand details like favicon and sharing image improve trust and make your landing pages look polished across channels.

## Where to find brand assets

1. Open your landing page in Builder.
2. Select **Settings** in the left toolbar.
3. In **Page Settings**, open **Brand assets**.

## Add or change your favicon

1. In **Favicon image**, upload or select your favicon file.
2. Save your changes.

Recommended format: **180 x 180px PNG**.

## Add or change your social sharing image

1. In **Social sharing image**, upload or select your image.
2. Save your changes and test a shared link preview.

Recommended format: **1200 x 630px**.

## Creative tips for better click-through

- Use high contrast and short headline text on sharing images.
- Keep visuals close to your ad angle and offer positioning.
- Avoid clutter so the preview stays readable on mobile.

> If you recently changed images, some platforms may still show a cached preview for a short period.

## Troubleshooting

**I still see the old favicon.**

- Refresh the page and clear browser cache, then check again.

**Social preview has not updated yet.**

- Link preview tools may cache images before showing the latest version.
