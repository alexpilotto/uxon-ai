## Article
Buttons are your primary conversion action. Use Builder's global button settings to keep CTA styling consistent across your landing page.

## Open button settings

1. Open your landing page in Builder.
2. Go to **Settings** -> **Buttons**.

## Set button colours

- **Primary background** and **Primary text**: main CTA style.
- **Secondary background** and **Secondary text**: lower-priority actions.
- **Link only text**: link-style CTA colour.

## Adjust typography and shape

- Choose font source and weight for CTA text.
- Enable uppercase when it fits your brand voice.
- Set border thickness, button shape, corner radius, and shadow strength.

## Conversion-focused button tips

- Keep your primary CTA visually stronger than secondary actions.
- Use wording and style that match your campaign intent.
- Check button readability in both desktop and mobile preview.

> Use **Quick setup** for a fast starting point, then fine-tune in full button settings.

## Troubleshooting

**Buttons blend into the background.**

- Increase contrast by adjusting primary background and text colour.

**Buttons look too cramped or too large.**

- Adjust button shape and padding settings, then recheck in mobile preview.
