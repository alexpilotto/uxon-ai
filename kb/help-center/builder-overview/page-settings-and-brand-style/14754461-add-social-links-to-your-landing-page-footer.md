## Article
Use Footer settings to add your social links once and keep profile URLs consistent across landing pages that include social icons.

## Open footer social settings

1. Open your landing page in Builder.
2. Go to **Settings** -> **Footer**.

## Add your social profile links

Paste profile URLs for the channels you use, including:

- Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, X, Pinterest
- WhatsApp, Reddit, Snapchat, WeChat, Spotify

Save after updating your links.

## When social links appear on the page

- Social links show in sections/templates that include social icon elements.
- If a section has no social icons, those links will not be visible there.

## Best practice

- Only add channels you actively manage.
- Keep link destinations current to avoid trust drops.
- Check icon visibility and contrast in mobile preview.

> A clean, up-to-date footer helps reinforce brand credibility at the end of the page journey.

## Troubleshooting

**I added links but no icons appear.**

- Use a footer or section template that includes social icons.

**A social link opens the wrong profile.**

- Review the exact URL in Footer settings and save again.
