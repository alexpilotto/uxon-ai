## Article
A fast preview routine helps you avoid broken experiences and protects conversion performance before launch.

## Use all preview modes

- **Desktop preview**: check hierarchy, spacing, and CTA visibility.
- **Mobile preview**: validate readability and tap flow.
- **Responsive preview**: review flexible behavior across screen sizes.

## Test interactions like a visitor

1. Enable **Interact with preview**.
2. Click main CTAs, menu links, and section jumps.
3. Submit test forms and verify expected behavior.

## Final check before handoff

1. Select **Save** in Builder.
2. Use the eye icon to **Open landing page** in a new tab.
3. Run one last end-to-end click-through on your live page URL.

> For paid traffic pages, mobile review is mandatory. Most issues that hurt conversion show up there first.

## Troubleshooting

**I cannot click elements in preview.**

- Enable **Interact with preview** first.

**Layout looks good on desktop but not on mobile.**

- Switch to **Mobile preview** and adjust section or element settings before saving.
