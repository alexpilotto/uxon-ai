## Article
Quick setup helps you set the visual foundation of your landing page in a few clicks.

## Open Quick setup

1. Open your landing page in Builder.
2. In the top bar, select **Quick setup**.

## What Quick setup configures

1. **Button shape**: choose Square, Rounded, or Pill.
2. **Button padding**: choose Tight, Default, or Airy.
3. **Image and block shape**: choose Square or Rounded.

Your selection updates core style settings so you can move faster with consistent design.

## After Quick setup

- Review your page in Desktop and Mobile preview.
- Open **Settings** if you want deeper control over colors, typography, and SEO.
- Save once the new style direction looks right.

> Best practice: run Quick setup first on a new variant, then fine-tune details in Page Settings.

## Troubleshooting

**I cannot find Quick setup.**

- Look in the Builder top bar next to the page and variant controls.

**I want more styling control than Quick setup offers.**

- Open **Settings** for full page-level controls.
