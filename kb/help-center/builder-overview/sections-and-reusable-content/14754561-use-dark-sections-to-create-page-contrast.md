## Article
Dark section styling helps key blocks stand out, especially on long-form landing pages where visual rhythm matters.

## Enable dark style on a section

1. Select the section in Builder.
2. Open **Section settings**.
3. Turn on **Invert colors** (dark section style).
4. Save and review in Desktop and Mobile preview.

## Where dark sections work best

- Social proof and testimonials
- Offer breakdown and pricing highlights
- Final CTA sections near page bottom

## Design tips

- Keep text contrast high for readability.
- Use dark sections selectively to avoid visual fatigue.
- Ensure button colors stay prominent on dark backgrounds.

> Contrast is a conversion tool. Use it to guide attention to high-value sections, not as decoration.

## Troubleshooting

**Text is hard to read after inverting colors.**

- Adjust global text/button colors or local section styling for stronger contrast.

**Section no longer matches page style.**

- Use dark style only on key sections and keep the rest of the page visually consistent.
