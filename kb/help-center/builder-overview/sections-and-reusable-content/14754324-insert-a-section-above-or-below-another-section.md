## Article
When structure matters, inserting sections relative to existing content is faster than moving them later.

## When to use this

- You want to add proof directly under a hero.
- You need to insert a CTA before pricing.
- You are refining flow after reviewing campaign traffic behavior.

## How to insert above or below

1. In the canvas, select the section near where you want to insert.
2. Use the blue **+** button at the top to **Insert section above**, or the bottom one to **Insert section below**.
3. In **Browse Sections**, choose the section template you want.
4. The new section is added in the exact position you selected.

## After inserting

- Open section settings and customize copy and visuals.
- Switch to **Mobile preview** to confirm spacing and reading flow.
- Save once the section order is final.

> If you add sections from the Layout panel instead, you may still need to reorder. Use insert-above/below when placement precision matters.

## Troubleshooting

**I cannot see the + insert buttons.**

- Select a section first in the canvas so the section overlay appears.

**The section went to an unexpected position.**

- Repeat the action from the target section, then confirm you clicked the top or bottom + control correctly.
