## Article
The **Browse Sections** modal helps you find the right section quickly, so you can build landing pages faster without guesswork.

## Open Browse Sections

1. In Builder, open **Layout**.
2. Click **Add**, or use the blue **+** insert controls on canvas.
3. The **Browse Sections** modal opens.

## Use search and filters

- **Search**: find templates by name.
- **Categories**: narrow to section types (for example heroes, testimonials, pricing).
- **Variant filter**: switch between template variants where available.

## Use My templates

- Open **My templates** to insert reusable sections your team has saved.
- This is useful for keeping consistent conversion patterns across campaigns.

## Selection tips for marketers

- Choose by section goal first (attention, proof, offer, CTA), then by style.
- Keep the page flow clear before adding visual complexity.

> If you are iterating quickly, insert first and polish later. Speed comes from getting structure right early.

## Troubleshooting

**I cannot find a section template.**

- Clear search, switch category to **All sections**, and check filters.

**The section inserted in the wrong place.**

- Use insert-above/below controls or drag in Layout to reorder.
