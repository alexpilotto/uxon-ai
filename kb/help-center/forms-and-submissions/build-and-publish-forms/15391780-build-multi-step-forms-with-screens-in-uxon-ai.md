## Article
Screens let you split a form into steps so visitors answer a focused set of questions at a time.

## Add a screen

1. Open the form in Form Builder.
2. In the left sidebar, find **Screens**.
3. Select **Screen** to add a new step.
4. Update the screen title and description from the canvas or screen settings.

## Configure screen content

Select a screen to open **Screen settings**. You can update:

- **Screen title**
- **Screen description**
- **Show screen title**
- **Show description**
- **Hide screen**
- **Next button label**
- **Back button label**

## Organize screens

Use the screen action menu in the sidebar or canvas to:

- **Move Up** or **Move Down** to change order.
- **Duplicate** to reuse a similar step.
- **Hide** to keep the screen in Builder without showing it to visitors.
- **Remove** to delete the screen.

## Add fields to a screen

1. Select the screen you want to edit.
2. Choose a field from the left sidebar, or select **Add field to screen** in the canvas.
3. Configure the new field from **Field settings**.

> Use hidden screens when you are testing structure during review. Remove screens only when you are sure they are no longer needed.

## Troubleshooting

**I cannot remove a screen.**

- A form must keep at least one screen.

**A screen does not appear for visitors.**

- Open **Screen settings** and confirm **Hide screen** is turned off.
