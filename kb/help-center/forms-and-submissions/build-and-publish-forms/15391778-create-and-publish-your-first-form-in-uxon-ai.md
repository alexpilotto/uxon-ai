Use forms to capture leads, qualify visitors, and send responses into your UXON AI workspace.

## Create a form

1. Go to **Dashboard** > **Forms**.
2. Select **Create form**.
3. UXON AI opens the new form in Form Builder.
4. Rename the form from **Form settings** so your team can identify it later.

## Publish the form

1. Build the form screens and fields.
2. Select **Publish** in the top-right corner.
3. After a form is already published, the same button changes to **Save** when there are new draft changes.

Publishing creates the version that can collect real submissions and appear in landing page form selectors.

## Preview a form

- Use the eye icon in Form Builder to open the form in a new tab.
- Use the eye icon in the Forms table to preview from the dashboard.

> Draft and archived forms are not available to visitors. Publish the form before sharing or embedding it.

## Manage forms from the dashboard

In **Dashboard** > **Forms**, each form row shows status, last edited time, submission count, and actions.

- **Edit**: reopen the form in Builder.
- **Submissions**: open response details for that form.
- **Duplicate**: create a draft copy.
- **Archive**: remove the form from live use without deleting it.
- **Delete**: permanently remove the form.

## Form statuses

- **Draft**: still being built and not available for live submissions.
- **Published**: available for visitors and landing pages.
- **Archived**: no longer available to visitors.

## Troubleshooting

**The form preview says unavailable.**

- Confirm the form is published and has not been archived.

**I do not see forms in my dashboard.**

- Check that you are in the correct workspace and client account.
