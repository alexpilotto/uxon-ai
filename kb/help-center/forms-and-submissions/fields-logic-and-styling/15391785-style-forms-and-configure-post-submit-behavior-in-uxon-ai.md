## Article
Use Form settings to control how your form looks and what happens after a visitor submits it.

## Open Form settings

1. Open the form in Form Builder.
2. Select **Form settings** above the canvas.

## General settings

- **Form title**: rename the form for your team and, if enabled, for visitors.
- **Show form title**: show or hide the form name above the screens.
- **Use question as screen heading**: on one-question screens, use the question label as the main heading.
- **Redirect after submission**: send visitors to a thank-you page or another URL after submit.

## Design settings

You can configure:

- Form background color
- Heading color
- Body content color
- Font
- Input rounding
- Button rounding
- Screen title size
- Question size
- Content size

## What happens after submission

- If redirect is off, visitors see the form success message.
- If redirect is on, visitors are sent to the configured redirect URL after a successful submit.

> Best practice: use a dedicated thank-you page when you need a clear next step, conversion confirmation, or additional tracking.

## Save design changes

Select **Publish** for a new form or **Save** for changes to an already published form.

## Troubleshooting

**The redirect does not happen.**

- Check that **Redirect after submission** is enabled and the redirect URL is complete, including https://.

**The form does not match my brand.**

- Review the design settings and confirm the form has been saved or published after changes.
