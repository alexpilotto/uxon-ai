## Article
Opportunities is built to help marketers decide where to focus landing page optimization first.

## Start with the right filters

- **Date range**: choose the period that matches your planning cycle.
- **Status**: compare **Active campaigns** or **All campaigns**.
- **Events**: select only the conversion events that matter for your goal.

## What each key metric means

| Metric | How to use it |
| --- | --- |
| **Spend** | Shows budget concentration. Higher spend usually means higher upside from improving conversion rate. |
| **Clicks** | Indicates traffic volume. More clicks can validate faster. |
| **Conversions** | Actual outcomes from selected conversion events. |
| **CVR** | Efficiency signal. Lower CVR with meaningful spend is often your first optimization target. |
| **Conversion Value** | Revenue or value impact for selected events. |
| **Score** | Quick ranking signal to help triage where to look first. |

## Simple prioritization framework

1. Prioritize campaigns with **high spend** and **below-target CVR**.
2. Protect and scale campaigns with strong CVR and strong value.
3. Treat low-volume campaigns as secondary unless they are strategic.

## Use Opportunity Details before launching tests

Select **View opportunity** to inspect campaign context and event breakdown. Use this context to define clearer landing page hypotheses.

> Best practice: review Opportunities weekly, then convert top opportunities into tests in your **Testing Plan**.
