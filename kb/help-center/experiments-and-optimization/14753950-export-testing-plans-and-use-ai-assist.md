## Article
Testing Plan includes two practical productivity tools: CSV export for reporting and AI assist for refining hypotheses and learnings.

## Export Testing Plan to CSV

1. Open **Testing Plan**.
2. Select the **Export CSV** button in the header.
3. Use the file for reporting, planning reviews, or stakeholder updates.

Export includes core planning fields and linked experiment context where available.

## Use AI assist for hypotheses

1. Open a test item.
2. In **Hypothesis**, select **Ask to modify**.
3. Describe the change you want, then apply the result.

## Use AI assist for learnings

1. Go to the **Results** tab inside the test.
2. In **Learnings**, select **Ask to modify**.
3. Refine tone, structure, or clarity before saving.

## Use templates to speed up writing

In both Hypothesis and Learnings fields, use **Start from template** to generate a strong first draft, then tailor it to your campaign context.

## Prompt tips for marketers

- Reference your target audience and offer.
- Specify the metric you want to improve (for example CVR or CPA).
- Ask for concise, testable language you can execute in one sprint.

## Troubleshooting

**CSV export takes time.**

- Large plans can take longer. Keep the page open until export completes.

**AI assist is unavailable.**

- Check workspace access and try again from the same test modal.
