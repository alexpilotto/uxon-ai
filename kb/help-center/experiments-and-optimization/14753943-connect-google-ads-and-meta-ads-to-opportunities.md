## Article
Opportunities helps you turn ad performance data into a focused landing page optimization plan. To start, connect your ad accounts.

## Before you connect

- Make sure you can access the Google Ads and/or Meta Ads accounts you want to analyze.
- Confirm you are in the correct UXON workspace and client account.
- If account connection is restricted in your workspace, ask your admin to connect accounts for you.

## How to connect ad accounts

1. Go to **Dashboard** -> **Opportunities**.
2. Select **Connect Ad Accounts**.
3. In the modal, choose **Connect** on **Google Ads** and/or **Meta Ads**.
4. Complete authorization with the selected platform.
5. Return to Opportunities after authorization completes.

## What happens after connection

- Platform status updates to **Connected**.
- If multiple ad accounts are available, UXON may ask you to pick which accounts to use.
- Once accounts are selected, campaign snapshots are imported for Opportunities analysis.

## Manage or disconnect connections

Use **Manage Ad Accounts** in Opportunities to select accounts, update selection, or disconnect a platform.

## Troubleshooting

**Connection succeeds but no Opportunities appear.**

- Open **Manage Ad Accounts** and confirm at least one account is selected.

**Connection expired or failed.**

- Disconnect and reconnect the platform, then refresh data.
