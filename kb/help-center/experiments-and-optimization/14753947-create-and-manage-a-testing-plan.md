## Article
Testing Plan helps you organize hypotheses, launch timing, and outcomes in one place so your optimization program stays consistent.

## How to add a test

1. Go to **Dashboard** -> **Testing Plan**.
2. Select **Add test**.
3. Complete required fields in **Details**:Status
4. Launch date
5. Test type
6. Primary goal
7. Hypothesis
8. Select **Add test** to save.

## Manage tests from the table

- Open a test by selecting its nickname or hypothesis.
- Use row actions to **Edit**, **Duplicate**, or **Delete**.
- Use pagination controls to move across pages of tests.

## Keep the plan execution-ready

- Use one primary goal per test.
- Write hypotheses as expected behavior change, not just design tasks.
- Set realistic launch months so the roadmap reflects real team capacity.

## Troubleshooting

**Test does not save.**

- Check required fields: status, launch date, test type, primary goal, and hypothesis.

**Actions are locked.**

- Your current plan may not include Testing Plan access. Ask your admin to update access.
