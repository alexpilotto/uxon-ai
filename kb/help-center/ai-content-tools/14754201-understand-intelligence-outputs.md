## Article
After a run completes, Intelligence organizes findings into practical sections you can use for campaign messaging and landing page optimization.

## Customer segments

This section highlights top audience groups by estimated size and intent signals.

- **Top desire**: what each segment wants most
- **Top pain**: key frustrations and blockers
- **Proof needed**: evidence required to increase trust

Use **Edit** to refine tags and shape your working messaging direction.

## Positioning snapshot

Compares your brand against selected competitors across positioning summary, strengths, and key limitations.

- Your row is marked with **You**.
- Use this table to find where your narrative is underplayed or overused.

## Messaging matrix

Shows how brands frame major claims side by side.

- Use it to identify repeated category language and whitespace opportunities.
- Prioritize sharper value framing where competitors sound similar.

## Category baseline vs ownable angles

- **Must match (baseline)**: claims expected by the market
- **Ownable angles**: differentiators with stronger upside

Use this section to balance credibility and differentiation on your landing pages.

## Review intelligence

Converts review data into messaging signals:

- Reviews analyzed and source breakdown
- Theme analytics (Brand, Product/Service, Support, Price)
- Where customers see strongest value
- Where customers feel friction
- Reviews by theme with drill-down details

## How to apply these insights

1. Translate top segment pain and proof needs into hero and section messaging.
2. Use ownable angles as hypothesis inputs for your testing plan.
3. Address top friction signals with clearer proof, risk reversal, and CTA support.

> Treat Intelligence as a decision accelerator: use it to focus execution, then validate outcomes with experiments and conversion metrics.
